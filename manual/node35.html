Credits and references

get_homologues.pl is designed, created and maintained at the http://www.eead.csic.es/compbioLaboratory of Computational Biology at Estación Experimental de Aula Dei/CSIC in Zaragoza (Spain) and at the http://www.ccg.unam.mx/ vinuesaCenter for Genomic Sciences of Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (CCG/UNAM).

The code was written mostly by Bruno Contreras-Moreira and Pablo Vinuesa, but it also includes code and binaries from http://www.orthomcl.orgOrthoMCL v1.4 (algorithm OMCL, -M), http://sourceforge.net/projects/cogtrianglesCOGtriangles v2.1 (algorithm COGS, -G), http://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.govNCBI Blast+, https://github.com/desmid/mviewMVIEW and http://www.bioperl.orgBioPerl 1.5.2.

Other contributors: Carlos P Cantalapiedra, Roland Wilhelm.

We ask the reader to cite the main references describing the get_homologues software,

  • Contreras-Moreira,B and Vinuesa,P (2013) GET_HOMOLOGUES, a versatile software package for scalable and robust microbial pangenome analysis. Appl.Environ.Microbiol. 79:7696-7701.
  • Vinuesa P and Contreras-Moreira B (2015) Robust Identification of Orthologues and Paralogues for Microbial Pan-Genomics Using GET_HOMOLOGUES: A Case Study of pIncA/C Plasmids. In Bacterial Pangenomics, Methods in Molecular Biology Volume 1231, 203-232, edited by A Mengoni, M Galardini and M Fondi.
